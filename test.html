Our Commitment to Affordable & Workforce Housing

Affordable and workforce housing is essential to address the growing housing crisis, ensuring that low- and middle-income families have access to safe and stable homes. This type of housing supports economic diversity and stability within communities, allowing essential workers like teachers, healthcare providers, and service workers to live near their workplaces.

As housing costs continue to rise, expanding affordable and workforce housing is crucial for reducing homelessness and fostering inclusive, resilient communities.
