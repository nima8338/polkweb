our story

Tanora Polk and Reggie Polk
Reggie Polk & Tanora Polk, the visionary Founders of Polk & Associates Construction Inc. (Polk), have been redefining excellence in the construction industry since 2011. Polk Construction is a powerhouse full-service firm specializing in Construction Management, General Contracting, Civil Construction, Facilities Maintenance, and Real Estate Development. With a dynamic leadership team and a passionate group of construction professionals, they deliver unparalleled expertise in estimating, project management, facilities operations, and innovative construction processes—offering solutions far beyond the reach of a typical general contractor.
